About The Divergent Movie Series

The Divergent Series is a trilogy of science fiction action films adapted from Veronica Roth's novels. These movies were produced by Summit Entertainment and Lionsgate Films. It takes place in a dystopian society and includes Divergent, Insurgent, and Allegiant.
They have been produced by Lucy Fisher, Pouya Shahbazian, and Douglas Wick.
